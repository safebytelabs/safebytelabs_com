About us

Safe Byte Labs is a cybersecurity firm based in Asturias, Spain. We research, develop and innovate on cybersecurity with our own software and hardware.

Mission

Our mission is to empower businesses by providing a secure, personalized, and adaptable environment for their operations.

Vision

Our vision is to work transparently based on the needs of the client, investing in the best resources and an incredible team, and focusing on generating long-term impact.

Values

Our values are:

a) Integrity: where our words and actions converge.
b) Responsibility: being serious without the need to wear a suit.
c) Inclusion: fostering a healthy relationship of trust among everybody.
d) Excellence: doing a few things very well is better than doing many things poorly.
e) Commitment to innovation: the future knows no boundaries.
f) Commitment to sustainability: we will accomplish great things, but not at any cost. We will avoid unnecessary impacts and use our knowledge and business to protect nature.
